A comprehensive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze customer retention, revenue performance, service quality, complaints, and churn behavior for a telecommunications business. The dashboard consolidates customer, revenue, complaint, and subscription data into interactive reports, enabling business leaders to monitor customer health and identify opportunities to improve retention and profitability.

The solution provides actionable insights into customer segmentation, churn trends, service quality, revenue leakage, complaint management, and plan performance through dynamic visualizations and business-focused KPIs. It empowers telecom stakeholders to make informed decisions that improve customer experience and maximize long-term business value.

---

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates how Microsoft Power BI can transform telecom customer data into meaningful business intelligence. The dashboard provides executives, customer success teams, revenue managers, and operations leaders with a centralized platform to monitor customer retention, evaluate churn risk, analyze complaint patterns, and assess revenue performance across different customer segments and subscription plans.

By integrating multiple business dimensions into a single analytics solution, the dashboard helps organizations proactively identify customer dissatisfaction, reduce churn, optimize subscription plans, and improve customer lifetime value through data-driven decision-making.

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Overview Dashboard

Provides a comprehensive summary of overall telecom business performance through high-level KPIs including Total Customers, Total Revenue, Churn Rate, Revenue Loss Percentage, and Average Customer Satisfaction. The dashboard combines monthly business trends, top-performing subscription plans, customer distribution by state, customer type distribution, and churn distribution to deliver an executive-level view of organizational performance.

### Customer Retention Dashboard

Focuses on customer retention and revenue health by monitoring Total Customers, Retention Rate, Churn Rate, ARPU, Revenue Trend, Customer Type distribution, Revenue Lost by Plan, and Satisfaction Score. This dashboard enables customer success teams to identify retention opportunities and evaluate the effectiveness of customer engagement strategies.

### Customer Analysis Dashboard

Provides detailed customer segmentation through age groups, gender distribution, customer types, geographic churn distribution, and churn behavior across different demographics. The dashboard helps identify customer segments with higher churn risk and supports targeted retention initiatives.

### Revenue Performance Dashboard

Analyzes revenue generation across subscription plans and customer types while monitoring Revenue Loss Rate, Revenue Lost, ARPU, Monthly Revenue Trends, and Plan-wise Revenue Performance. It enables business leaders to evaluate financial performance, identify revenue leakage, and optimize pricing strategies.

### Service Quality & Complaint Analysis

Monitors customer complaints, complaint rates, complaint categories, customer satisfaction, and monthly complaint trends. The dashboard helps service teams evaluate complaint distribution across plans and complaint types while identifying operational areas requiring service improvements.

## Dataset

The dashboard is built using a telecom customer analytics dataset containing customer demographics, subscription plans, customer type, geographic information, revenue transactions, churn status, customer satisfaction scores, complaint records, join dates, and service usage information.

The dataset supports customer retention analysis, revenue optimization, service quality monitoring, complaint management, and business performance reporting across multiple operational dimensions within the telecommunications industry.
